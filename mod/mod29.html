Key words: Unit data modification
This mod changes the endgame for all nine Indian nations, giving them unique and expensive technologies to better prepare them for a push against a fortified European town. New technologies, new units. Intended to speed up the conclusions of long games versus the AI, and better highlight every faction's unique aspects. In addition, some minor issues in the game, such as technologies reporting incorrect information about their benefits, have also been resolved.
